What is Friends#unhosted?

Friends#unhosted is an alternative social network for those of us who want to own our social network data. With F#U, your data is not stored at a central server, but at a place of your choosing. You can even host the data server on your home network, given that the server is reachable from the internet. You can start a server in the cloud, or you can choose one of many online storages Link Link Link. To add a friend, you need to get your friends storage address, which looks like an email address: username@someunhostedserver.com. Then you add that to your social network.
